Modern Dental Anesthetic

Comfort comes first. At Ars Dental, we use modern anesthetic techniques to keep you relaxed and pain‑free during treatment—from simple fillings to more advanced procedures. We tailor the approach to your needs and medical history.

Types of Anesthesia

  • • Topical gel: numbs the surface before injections or cleanings in sensitive areas
  • • Local anesthesia: targeted numbing for fillings, extractions, and most procedures
  • • Light oral sedation: optional support for anxious patients (by assessment)

What’s Included

  • • Medical history review and safety checklist
  • • Gentle delivery techniques and pre‑numbing gel
  • • Ongoing comfort checks during treatment
  • • Post‑treatment instructions and follow‑up

Who Benefits?

  • • Patients with dental anxiety or previous discomfort
  • • Sensitive teeth or inflamed gums
  • • Longer procedures (multiple fillings, surgical care)
  • • Children or first‑time visitors

Before & After Anesthesia

FAQs

Will I feel pain? You may feel pressure but not sharp pain. Tell us at any time—your comfort is our priority.

Is it safe? Yes. We review your medical history, use precise dosing, and monitor your comfort throughout.

How long does numbness last? Typically 1–3 hours depending on the area and dose.
