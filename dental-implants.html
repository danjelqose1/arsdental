Dental Implants

Dental Implants: Natural Look, Lasting Strength

Dental implants are the most durable and natural‑looking way to replace missing teeth. At Ars Dental, titanium implants are placed in the jawbone where they integrate and support a crown, bridge, or full arch—restoring function and confidence.

Treatment Options

  • • Single‑tooth implant: replaces one missing tooth without touching neighbours
  • • Implant bridge: replaces several teeth with fewer implants
  • • Full‑arch (All‑on‑4/6): fixed teeth on 4–6 implants per jaw

What’s Included

  • • 3D assessment and planning (x‑rays/CBCT as required)
  • • Implant placement under local anesthesia with comfort measures
  • • Healing abutment and follow‑ups during osseointegration
  • • Final crown/bridge with precise bite adjustment
  • • Home‑care and maintenance plan

Who Is a Candidate?

  • • Missing one or more teeth
  • • Healthy gums and adequate bone (or eligible for grafting)
  • • Non‑smoker or willing to reduce smoking
  • • Good oral hygiene and regular dental visits

FAQs

Is implant surgery painful? It’s performed under local anesthesia; most patients report only mild soreness afterward.

How long do implants last? With proper care and maintenance, implants can last decades or even a lifetime.

What if I don’t have enough bone? Bone grafting or sinus lift may be recommended; we’ll review options during your exam.

Who Benefits Most

  • • One or more missing teeth
  • • Loose partials or uncomfortable dentures
  • • Desire for fixed, natural‑looking teeth
  • • Good overall health and hygiene

After‑Care Tips

  • • Soft diet for the first days after surgery
  • • Keep the area clean; use prescribed rinses
  • • No smoking during healing
  • • Brush and floss daily (use floss threaders)
  • • Regular checkups and hygiene visits
