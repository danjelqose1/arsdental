Tooth Braces

Tooth Braces: Straighten & Align

Braces align teeth and correct bite issues for a healthier, more confident smile. At Ars Dental, we offer metal and ceramic options, planned with digital imaging for predictable results and clear timelines.

Treatment Options

  • • Metal braces: durable and efficient—great for most cases
  • • Ceramic (tooth‑coloured): discreet brackets that blend with your smile
  • • Clear aligners: removable trays for select cases

What’s Included

  • • Orthodontic assessment and imaging
  • • Personalized plan and estimated timeline
  • • Bracket/aligner start and instructions
  • • Regular adjustments and hygiene guidance
  • • Retainers and long‑term retention plan

Who’s a Candidate?

  • • Crowding or spacing
  • • Overbite, underbite, or crossbite
  • • Rotated teeth or midline shifts
  • • Teens and adults seeking predictable, long‑term results

Typical Timeline

FAQs

Do braces hurt? Expect mild pressure for a few days after adjustments—it’s normal and manageable.

Can adults get braces? Yes. Many adults choose ceramic braces or aligners for discreet treatment.

How do I clean around braces? We’ll show you interdental brushes, floss threaders, and techniques to keep gums healthy.

Who Benefits Most

  • • Crowding or spacing
  • • Bite issues (over/under/crossbite)
  • • Shifting teeth after previous treatment
  • • Teens and adults seeking alignment

After‑Care Tips

  • • Brush after meals; use interdental brushes
  • • Avoid very hard/sticky foods
  • • Wear elastics/retainers as instructed
  • • Keep adjustment visits every 4–8 weeks
  • • Use orthodontic wax for minor irritation
